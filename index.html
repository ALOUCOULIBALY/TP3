THE PRATICAL WORK 3 : THE SOCIAL POLICIES AND INEQUALITIES

The living environments equity index is a new tool for assessing the state of disadvantage in the city's neighborhoods, to restore “equity” in the territory.

Living environments equity index

The living environments equity index aims to locate living environments with cumulative urban vulnerabilities in order to encourage the prioritization and convergence of municipal investments. This index represents environments that combine social, economic, environmental vulnerabilities, access to local resources, access to cultural, sport and leisure resources and urban security.
